QR Entry/Exit

What to do when QR scan does not work.

Booking

  • 1. Go to Find Parking and select your preferred lot and slot.
  • 2. Choose start and end time, then confirm reservation.
  • 3. Complete payment to activate QR-based gate entry.
  • 4. For cancellation, open My Reservations and click Cancel.

Payment

  • 1. Supported methods: Card, UPI, Wallet.
  • 2. On successful payment, receipt and QR ticket are generated.
  • 3. If payment fails, retry from My Reservations.
  • 4. For charge issues, share reservation code with support team.

QR Entry / Exit

  • 1. Show reservation QR at entry gate for check-in.
  • 2. Show same QR at exit gate for checkout and completion.
  • 3. If scan fails, use reservation code manually at gate scanner.
  • 4. Entry works only after successful payment.

Privacy Policy (Short)

We use your account, booking, payment and gate activity data only for service delivery, security, and analytics. We do not sell personal data. Access is role-based for admins/owners where required.

Terms of Service (Short)

Bookings are subject to slot availability and payment success. Misuse of QR entry, duplicate bookings, or fraudulent payments may lead to account restrictions. Timing and cancellation policies apply as shown in booking flow.
